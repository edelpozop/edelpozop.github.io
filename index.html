About

Elias Del Pozo Puñal is a post-doctoral researcher in High Performance Computing (HPC) at the Polytechnic University of Madrid (UPM), where he completed his PhD in 2025 with the highest distinction (cum laude). He is a member of the Computer Architecture Research Group (ARCOS) at Universidad Carlos III de Madrid.

His research activity focuses mainly on High Performance Computing, computing continuum, distributed systems, file systems, Internet of Things (IoT), and simulation of edge and fog environments. This has allowed him to participate in multiple national research projects and collaborate with leading institutions in the field.

As a result of his research, he has authored and co-authored more than 10 journal and conference articles, with expertise in scalable data management, distributed systems, and edge computing platforms. His work has been published in prestigious journals such as Future Generations Computer Systems and IEEE Access.
